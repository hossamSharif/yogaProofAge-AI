Gua Sha Sculpting

5 min

How-To

Begin with a clean face and apply a few drops of facial oil to help the tool glide smoothly. Holding the gua sha tool at a 15-degree angle to the skin, gently scrape in an upward and outward motion. Start from the center of your face and move towards your hairline. Repeat each stroke 3-5 times.

Pro Tips

  • check_circle Always use gentle pressure; the goal is to massage, not to cause discomfort.
  • check_circle Drink a glass of water after your session to help flush out toxins.
  • check_circle Don't forget the neck area! Scrape from the collarbone upwards to the jawline.
