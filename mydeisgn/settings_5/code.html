Getting Started

How do I create an account? expand_more

To create an account, download the app from the App Store or Google Play. Open the app and tap "Sign Up". You can sign up using your email address, Google, or Apple account. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your profile.

How does the skin analysis work? expand_more

Our AI-powered skin analysis uses your phone's camera to take a photo of your skin. The AI then analyzes the image for various skin concerns like acne, texture, hydration, and pigmentation to provide you with a personalized skin report.

Using the App

How do I build my skincare routine? expand_more

After your initial skin analysis, the app will recommend a personalized routine. You can customize this routine by adding or removing products from our extensive database. Go to the 'Routine' tab to view and edit your morning and evening routines.

Can I track my progress? expand_more

Yes! The 'Progress' tab allows you to track your skin's improvement over time. We encourage you to take regular photos and log your product usage. The app will generate charts and visual timelines to show your progress.

How do I find product information? expand_more

Use the 'Explore' tab to search for products. You can scan a product's barcode or search by name. Each product page includes a detailed ingredient analysis, user reviews, and information on its suitability for your skin type.

Account & Subscription

How do I manage my subscription? expand_more

You can manage your subscription directly through the App Store (for iOS) or Google Play Store (for Android). Navigate to your profile settings within the app and tap on 'Manage Subscription' for a direct link.
